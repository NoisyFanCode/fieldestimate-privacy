Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the FieldEstimate mobile application developed by NoisyFanCode.

Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy explains how the FieldEstimate mobile application (“the App”) handles information. The App is developed and published by NoisyFanCode (“Developer”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). FieldEstimate is a construction material estimation calculator that allows users to perform calculations and save project estimates locally on their device. 1. Information Collection The App is designed to minimize data collection. Personal Information FieldEstimate does not collect personal information, including: names email addresses phone numbers account information payment information project data The App does not require user accounts. Project and Calculation Data Any data created inside the App, including: project folders calculation inputs generated estimates exported files (PDF or CSV) is stored locally on the user’s device only. This information is not transmitted to the developer and not stored on external servers. Users are responsible for managing and backing up their own exported files. 2. Crash Reporting and Diagnostics To improve stability and performance, FieldEstimate uses Firebase Crashlytics, a crash reporting service provided by Google. Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects anonymous diagnostic information when the app crashes or encounters errors. This may include: device model operating system version app version crash stack traces diagnostic information related to the crash This data does not include: names email addresses project data calculation inputs any personally identifiable information. Crash data is used solely to identify bugs and improve app reliability. For more information about how Google processes Crashlytics data, see: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy 3. Use of Information The limited diagnostic data collected via Firebase Crashlytics is used only to: identify application crashes diagnose technical issues improve application stability improve future versions of the App The Developer does not sell, rent, or share user data. 4. Third-Party Services The App uses the following third-party service: Firebase Crashlytics Provided by Google LLC Purpose: crash reporting application diagnostics Privacy information: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are currently used. 5. Advertising and Tracking FieldEstimate currently: does not display advertisements does not track users across apps or websites does not use advertising identifiers for tracking 6. Data Security Because project data is stored locally on the user’s device, the Developer does not have access to it. Users are responsible for securing their own devices and managing their exported files. While reasonable efforts are made to maintain application security and stability, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. 7. Children’s Privacy FieldEstimate is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact the Developer so the issue can be addressed. 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy will be revised. Continued use of the App after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. 9. Contact Information If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, you may contact the Developer: NoisyFanCode Email: noisyfancode@outlook.com Summary FieldEstimate is designed with a privacy-first approach: No accounts required No personal data collection Project data stored locally on the device Anonymous crash diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics only
