Purpose of the Application

FieldEstimate provides construction material estimation calculators intended for general informational and planning purposes.

The application allows users to estimate quantities of construction materials based on measurements and inputs provided by the user.

Examples of calculators included in the application may include:

  • concrete
  • wall framing
  • drywall
  • roofing
  • siding
  • paint
  • trim / casing
  • railing

Estimates Only

All results generated by the application are estimates only.

Material quantities may vary depending on many factors including:

  • construction methods
  • measurement accuracy
  • site conditions
  • waste factors
  • material specifications
  • local building practices

Users must independently verify all quantities before purchasing materials or beginning construction work.
